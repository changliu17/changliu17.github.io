About me

2019-12-29

Hi,

I am Chang Liu, a market researcher who is now a graduate student at Boston University, concentrating on marketing communication research.
My background in the finance industry taught me the importance of people’s opinions and data, so I decided to transfer my skill set about statistics to market research. I refine my skill set about quantitative and qualitative research by working in market research companies and chasing a master’s degree in this field.

I recently become interested in UX research. Because I want to improve people’s life experience. I took an online course about UX design so I can better understand how researchers and designers working together to create better products.

I always have passion for the entertainment industry and I try to apply my skill set to this industry. I love reality show so I writing scripts and creating marketing plans for one. I love TV series so I became a TV market researcher, observing programs from an industry angle. I also write articles about American TV series in Chinese, introducing more good TV series to wider audiences.

Thank you for visting my portfolio site!
